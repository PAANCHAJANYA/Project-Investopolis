ABOUT FEATURES CREATORS

Investopolis is a decentralized investment platform built on a private permissionless blockchain network. This platform acts as a one-stop solution for investment in budding start-ups in the country even if they are currently unlisted in the stock market, real-estate, and artworks via NFTs. The platform encompasses customer loyalty programs in order to encourage the investors and other stakeholders on the network to perform various actions. The investors and ideators on the platform are rewarded for various actions with tokens. As the utility of a token increases, the value of the token shall rise. The rewards the user can claim depends upon the number of tokens they collected and their utility. Every transaction performed on the platform shall have a 1% transaction fee. The user can pay by cryptocurrency in the network or barter a token he/she was rewarded prior.

Investment in Start-up Shares

Ideators admitted into the network share the details and vision of their start-up company, and periodically update their company valuation. Investors can analyse the company's growth overtime, communicate with them and after careful consideration can proceed further to buy their shares. He can later resale the shares at their then-market value.

Investment in Real-Estate

Users of the network can act as a seller/buyer in a property transaction (currently limited to lands). The platform acts as an intermediary and a registry for the same and enables smooth transactions mitigating the chances of transaction frauds and illegal occupancies.

Investment in NFTs

Users of the network can post their digital arts, photography, paintings, on the network and mint them as NFTs (Non Fungible Tokens). Users who are interested in gaining rights to the aforementioned images can quote a price and obtain the NFT.
