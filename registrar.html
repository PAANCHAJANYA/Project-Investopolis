Registration/Transfer Verifications

Hello Sir/Ma'am!

You are given the authority to enact a very important task to perform verification of property transactions.

In case of registration of already existing properties on Investopolis, simple verification online is sufficient.

In case of Transfer of Ownership transaction, as and when users of this platform submit the details of their transaction such as details of the parties involved, agreement conditions and clauses, and property details, a slot will alloted to them to visit your SRO i.e., Sub-Registrar Office

After the verification of all the details submitted via the platform and performing E-KYC during the alloted slot, you can verify the transaction to make it successful.
