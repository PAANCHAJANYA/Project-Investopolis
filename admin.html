# Admin

The Verification Stage

Aye Aye, Captain!

You are given the authority to enact a very important responsibility towards the Investopolis platform.

In addition to addressing various issues faced by the participants of this platform, you also have to verify new users and admit them into the network if and when successfully verified.

Although the concept of an administrator may seem to hint that there is centralization of some sort in this decentralized platform, it isn't! As long as a considerable number of administrators strive towards the maintenance of the Investopolis network, it will continue to show its decentralized nature.
