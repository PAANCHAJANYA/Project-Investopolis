About Investopolis

Investopolis is an investment platform encompassing customer loyalty through blockchain wherein investors can invest upon ideators' companies which are unlisted in the stock market but would have high market value when deployed.

The investors on the platform are rewarded for various actions with tokens. As the utility of a token increases, the value of the token shall rise.

Every transaction an investor performs shall have a 1% transaction fee. He/she can pay by cryptocurrency or use a token he/she was rewarded prior.

In addition to transactions from investors to ideators, investors can transfer tokens/cryptocurrency to other investors as well.
